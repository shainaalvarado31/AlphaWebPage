Events & Activities

Alpha Computer Association promotes participation, leadership, collaboration, and student engagement through educational workshops, meetings, and fundraising initiatives.

Our Events

Member Meeting

This activity encouraged active participation from Alpha members in the nomination and selection process for the new student board. It promoted leadership, collaboration, and involvement within the organization.

Brownie Pre-Sale

Alpha organized a brownie pre-sale as a fundraising initiative to support student activities and future events. This activity helped strengthen teamwork, planning, and promotion skills within the organization.

Business Intelligence en Acción

This educational event introduced students to Business Intelligence concepts, including analytical maturity, data governance, and emerging trends. It also connected students with professional experiences related to technology and analytics.
