Mission

Alpha Computer Association is committed to promoting academic growth, leadership, collaboration, and technological development among students. Through workshops, meetings, fundraising initiatives, and educational activities, Alpha creates opportunities for members to strengthen their skills and prepare for future careers in technology and computing.

Vision

Our vision is to become a recognized student organization that inspires innovation, teamwork, and professional growth within the university community. Alpha seeks to connect students with meaningful experiences, strengthen interest in technology, and encourage active participation in academic and professional opportunities.

Fun Fact

Alpha Computer Association has its own mascot named Pablito 🐧. Pablito is a friendly penguin who represents our community, teamwork, and passion for technology. You'll often see him in our events, promotions, and activities, bringing energy and fun to Alpha!

Hands-on Workshops

Alpha members actively participate in hands-on workshops where they develop technical skills such as programming, hardware setup, and problem-solving in real-world scenarios.
